Jopenkerk

At Jopenkerk, history and coziness come together. This old church in the heart of Haarlem has been transformed into a unique place where you can enjoy artisanal Jopen beers and flavorful dishes. While experiencing the special atmosphere, we invite you to participate in our Restaurant Quiz & Puzzle Hunt. Bastijan

BastiJan is a restaurant full of character and warmth in the culinary Lange Veerstraat of Haarlem. In a romantic setting with art deco and Art Nouveau, you'll enjoy dishes that are prepared with love and care. From tender chateaubriand to fried shrimp with garlic – chef Jan's specialty for 35 years. Here you come not only for the food, but also for the cozy atmosphere and the personal attention. Perfect for a night out where taste and experience come together.

Restaurant fris

At the edge of Frederikspark you'll find Fris, where modern fine dining meets casual elegance. Chef Eddie Meijboom and maître Julius Wever combine bold Asian and French flavors with seasonal ingredients. In a warm and sleek interior, you'll taste dishes that surprise and inspire. Whether you come for a romantic dinner or a business lunch, at Fris you'll experience a culinary adventure that is both refined and accessible.

Ratatouille Food & Wine

Ratatouille Food & Wine brings modern French cuisine to Haarlem with finesse and passion. This restaurant, awarded a Michelin star, stands for refinement and quality. Chef Jozua Jaring creates dishes where tradition and creativity come together. Every bite is a taste experience that takes you on a culinary journey. Whether you choose an extensive menu or an intimate dinner, here you'll taste the art of French gastronomy in a relaxed and modern atmosphere.

Toujours

Toujours is a restaurant in Haarlem where French elegance and coziness go hand in hand. Here you'll enjoy classic dishes with a modern twist, prepared with fresh ingredients and attention to detail. The atmosphere is warm and inviting, perfect for a relaxed evening with friends or a special moment with your loved one. At Toujours, everything revolves around quality, taste and the art of good food in a relaxed and stylish ambiance.

Metzo

Metzo offers a Mediterranean dining experience in the heart of Haarlem, where southern warmth and coziness are central. Enjoy fresh dishes with influences from Greece, Italy and Spain, prepared with authentic flavors and high-quality ingredients. The atmosphere is lively and relaxed, perfect for a cozy evening with friends or family. At Metzo you'll experience the sun of the Mediterranean Sea, combined with the hospitality of Haarlem.

De Beren

At De Beren, you've been enjoying lots of delicious food since 1984 in a cozy and accessible atmosphere. This family-friendly restaurant is known for its wide selection, good service and excellent price-quality ratio. Whether you come for lunch, dinner or just a snack in between, De Beren ensures that everyone feels welcome. It's the perfect place for families, friends and everyone who loves good food without fuss.

Restaurant Rood

Restaurant Rood is a hidden gem in Haarlem where passion for food and hospitality come together. In an intimate and stylish setting, you'll taste dishes that are composed with attention and creativity. The menu offers a beautiful mix of classic and contemporary flavors, with focus on fresh ingredients and seasonal products. Whether you choose an intimate dinner or a festive occasion, at Rood you'll experience culinary enjoyment in a warm and personal atmosphere.
